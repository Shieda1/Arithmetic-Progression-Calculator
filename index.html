What is the arithmetic progression?
The arithmetic progression is a sequence of numbers such that the difference between the consecutive terms is constant. sometimes it is also called the arithmetic sequence.
How to calculate arithmetic progression?
To calculate arithmetic progression you should use the following formula:
an = a1 + (n - 1) d
where:
an = the nth term in the sequence
a1 = the first term in the sequence
d = the common difference between terms
Why use arithmetic progression?
The arithmetic progression is used in order to generalise a set of patterns that we observe in our day to day life.
